Company Name: Air Squared

Title of Position: Quality Inspector (Weekend or 2nd Shift)

Position Type: Full-Time


Pay Range: $19-28/hour including shift premium, based on qualifications and experience. 2nd shift premium is $1/hr. Weekend shift premium is 12% of the hourly rate


Benefits include:

  • Health Insurance through Aetna (optional HSA plan with $10 per pay period matching)
  • $400 Flexible Spending Account, paid by employer
  • Long Term Disability Insurance, paid by employer
  • 3% matching SIMPLE IRA retirement plan
  • Safety shoe allowance
  • 3-weeks PTO accrual and paid holidays
  • Parental Leave: 1-week paid Bonding Leave, 4-weeks paid Medical Child-Birth Recovery Leave
  • Educational reimbursement

Location: Broomfield, Colorado


As a covered federal contractor, under Executive Order 14042, all employees at Air Squared are required to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19, regardless of position, or file a medical or religious exemption.


If selected, you will be required to be vaccinated against COVID-19 and submit documentation of proof of vaccination by December 8, 2021, or before onboarding with Air Squared, if after December 8, 2021. Air Squared will provide additional information regarding what information or documentation will be needed and how you can request a legally required exception from this requirement.


Air Squared Manufacturing, Inc. is an OEM manufacturer of scroll type compressors, vacuum pumps and expanders for the medical, aerospace, fuel cell and power generation industries. Air Squared offers exciting career opportunities, working on projects like the NASA Moxie project, Lockheed Martin, Chrysler, GM, Honeywell and other cutting-edge projects.


We are seeking a Quality Inspector to support the machining and manufacturing processes on the Weekend Day Shift or on 2nd shift during the week.


1st Shift: Monday - Thursday 5:30 am - 4:00 pm.

2nd Shift: Monday - Thursday 3:30 pm - 2:00 am. 2nd shift premium is $1/hr.

Weekend shift: Friday – Sunday 7:00 am – 7:30 pm. Weekend shift premium is 12% of the hourly rate.


Job Responsibilities:

  • Routinely inspect incoming and in-process parts by utilizing programmable CMMs, optical measurement systems and other inspection tools
  • Support NCR process for incoming parts and FAIR process for incoming/in-process parts
  • Support inspections for Operations and Warehouse as needed
  • Read and understand part prints including GD&T
  • Work independently and manage time to ensure the flow of production
  • Support inspections for Operations and Warehouse as needed
  • Use SPC as required
  • Keep work area organized at all times
  • Regularly lift 30 lbs. of raw materials or machine parts with or without accommodation
  • Perform other tasks as assigned

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or GED (required)
  • Prior experience as a Quality Inspector in an ISO 9001 machining environment (preferred)
  • Experience with manual inspection/measurement tools such as micrometers, calipers and gauges (preferred)
  • Ability to use programmable Zeiss CMM (with CALYPSO software) (preferred)
  • GD&T training and/or certification (preferred)
  • Employment with Air Squared is contingent on passing a statewide background check, drug screening (for substances illegal in the state of Colorado), and employment verification through E-Verify.
